Now Brewing – Application Developers! #tobeapartner

From the beginning, Starbucks set out to be a different kind of company. One that not only celebrated coffee and the rich tradition, but that also brought a feeling of connection. We are known for developing extraordinary leaders who share this passion and are guided by their service to others.

As an Application Developer, you’ll deliver and support technology solutions that enable business capabilities—partnering across IT and the business to analyze requirements, design and build software, automate testing, and deploy reliable solutions that perform well in production.

As an Application Developer, you will…

  • Build and deliver software solutions using established design patterns and coding standards—focused on secure, scalable, durable, and accessible outcomes.
  • Contribute across the delivery lifecycle (requirements/backlog refinement, technical design, development, test automation, and deployment) in Agile and/or waterfall environments.
  • Support production stability by participating in on-call support, troubleshooting incidents, contributing to root-cause analysis, tracking operational KPIs, and maintaining clear documentation for operations and users.

We’d Love To Hear From People With

  • 0–4 years of professional industry experience with software development
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field

Preferred Qualifications

  • Proficiency with relevant development languages, tools, frameworks, and dependencies, and strong fundamentals in data structures/algorithms
  • Experience building or integrating with established APIs, including common system integration patterns
  • Strong engineering habits: writing reusable, maintainable code, automated tests, and clear technical documentation
  • Strong communication and problem-solving skills; ability to balance priorities and meet deadlines
